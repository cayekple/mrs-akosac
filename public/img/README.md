## Image Guidelines:

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Recommended size:** 800x800 pixels minimum for quality display
- **Aspect ratio:** Square or portrait orientation works best
- **File size:** Keep under 2MB per image for optimal loading times

## Adding Your Images:

Simply copy your images to this directory with the exact filenames listed above, or update the filenames in `src/App.tsx` (galleryCategories section, lines 18-42) to match your image filenames.
